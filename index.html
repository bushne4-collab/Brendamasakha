About Me

I am a professional civil engineer specialising in road design, drainage systems and BIM coordination. I use modern tools to deliver efficient, sustainable infrastructure solutions that work in the model and in the ground.

My focus is on buildable designs — alignments a contractor can set out from, hydraulics that survive the first real storm, and quantities that hold up when the bills are priced. BIM coordination is part of how I ensure the road, drainage and structural disciplines agree before anything is poured.
